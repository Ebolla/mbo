Membership

Welcome to the membership page. Here you will be able to build incentive-driven loyalty programs for your customers. You can also assign rewards to each level of loyalty.

  • Basic 0 - 15% 10 999 customers
  • Bronze 15 - 30% 8 547 customers
  • Plus 30 - 35%
  • Silver 35 - 55% 3 520 customers
  • Plus 55 - 60%
  • Gold 60 - 80% 1 389 customers
  • Platinum 80 - 95% 543 customers
  • Plus 95 - 100%

Rewards

You may add rewards to any membership level. Remember that the idea is to give more valuable rewards as the membership levels raises. You may add general or personal offers to each membership level.
